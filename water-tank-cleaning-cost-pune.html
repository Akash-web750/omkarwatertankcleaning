How Often Should Water Tanks Be Cleaned?

Experts recommend cleaning water tanks at least once every six months. However, if your building experiences muddy water supply, construction activity nearby or heavy water usage, cleaning may be required more frequently.

Signs Your Water Tank Needs Immediate Cleaning

  • Bad smell from water
  • Yellow or muddy water
  • Visible algae inside the tank
  • Sediments in buckets or taps
  • Unusual taste in drinking water
  • Frequent plumbing blockages

Ignoring these warning signs can affect the health of your family and may also damage plumbing systems.

Best Time To Clean Water Tanks In Pune

The ideal time to clean a water tank is before and after the monsoon season. During monsoons, dust, mud, algae and contaminants can enter water storage systems more easily.

Regular cleaning before the rainy season helps maintain water quality and reduces the risk of bacterial growth inside the tank.
